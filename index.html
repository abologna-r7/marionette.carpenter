Carpenter

Carpenter is a library for building tables with sorting, pagination, and anything else you can imagine. It's built on top of Backbone and Marionette. Think of it like DataTables for the client-side MVC set.

Building tables should be easy.

Building a tabular representation of a JSON resource that enables sorting and pagination should require a minimal amount of code. We did put a man on the moon, after all! Try your hand at adding a column to the table below:
